Mario Kart Character Analysis

I've always wondered which Mario Kart Character to pick, to suit my style or counter an opponent. The method used here is amazing, showcasing something as simple as Pareto Frontier can be used to shortlist your options, knowing they're optimal for the situation. You can train a DeepNet to be able to play Mario Kart and beat everyone, but would if it can also give you it's Pareto Frontier say for character selection, or anything else, then it'd be a much more reliable tool.
